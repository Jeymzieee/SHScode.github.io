Cuisine got you

Today's Filipino cuisine is a direct blend of locally sourced products, flavors, and foreign influences. The Philippines' varied topography and extensive indigenous history are reflected in the regional adaptations of well-known foods that are found throughout the country. Filipino cuisine combines flavors that are sweet, salty, and sour. Since we are in Southeast Asia, rice plays a big role.
